# I Call Your Name

My tears have traced  
the passing years,  
each drop, a song  
the darkness hears.  

You said the light  
would heal the pain,  
but shadows found me  
once again.  

I breathe your scent  
where first we met,  
it stirs the ground,  
I can't forget.  

Your touch remains  
where sorrow sleeps,  
I call your name—  
and silence keeps.  

When silence folds  
your whispered name,  
the night remembers  
just the same.  

No hand to hold,  
no heart is near,  
I call your name—  
and none can hear.  

My soul fades slowly  
out of sight,  
the rain still veils  
the endless night.  

The roses wilt,  
their colors fade,  
as life becomes  
the death it made.  

I call your name,  
but no reply,  
just hollow stars  
across the sky.  

The wind repeats  
what hearts conceal—  
a wound that time  
will never heal.  

When silence folds  
your whispered name,  
the night remembers  
just the same.  

No hand to hold,  
no heart is near,  
I call your name—  
and none can hear.
